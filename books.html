Lucy's Library

I love books about infectious disease!! Well, more accurately, I love reading about infectious diseases. In reality, I have many problems with many books, both petty and genuine. But I really do enjoy well-researched, well-written books by smart people who try. This is my living collection of infectious disease non-fiction books I've read, the smart and the stupid, the compassionate and the stupid. You can click on the covers to see my full thoughts, and on the buttons below to filter/resort the books.
